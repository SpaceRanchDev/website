P2Pigeon: Peer to Peer Instant Group Encrypted Online Network

Our history dates back to 5th century BC, when Cyrus the Great established the first network of carrier pigeons. These winged messengers were used to carry information across vast distances, enabling communication between the farthest regions of his empire. Fast forward to the present day, and our team has taken inspiration from these feathered pioneers to create a revolutionary communication service known as P2Pigeon

Like the carrier pigeons of old, P2Pigeon is designed to be resilient, reliable, and able to operate in the face of adversity. Just as the birds were able to navigate treacherous terrain and harsh weather conditions to deliver their messages, P2Pigeon is built to withstand attempts by corporations and other entities to censor, disrupt, or intercept communications.
